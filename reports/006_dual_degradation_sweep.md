## Finding

This is the first stress test where both sides of the cue can degrade. It gives
the clearest boundary so far:

- Exact content remains strong, but wrong/noisy temporal cues can still flip it
  at high temporal beta.
- Noisy content is recoverable when temporal evidence is at least partially
  correct.
- Ambiguous family-blend content is recoverable only when temporal evidence
  points toward the correct lived neighborhood.
- Wrong content (`wrong_ladder`) can be rescued by correct temporal evidence at
  temporal beta `4+`.
- Pure noise content cannot be rescued reliably by temporal evidence alone.

Architectural implication: the coupled energy read works when at least one side
of the cue has real contact with the target and the other side is not strongly
misleading. It does not create identity from nothing. That is good: no hidden
controller, no magical recovery. The landscape needs an anchor.

The new failure mode is also clear: high temporal beta makes wrong temporal
context dangerous. This points toward future query-shaped temperature rather
than a fixed temporal weight. Strong temporal pressure should come from
engaged, coherent temporal evidence, not from a global knob.
